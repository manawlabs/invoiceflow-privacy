01

Overview

InvoiceFlow ("the App") is a free invoice management application built for freelancers, creators, and small business owners in India. We take your privacy seriously.

The short version: InvoiceFlow stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal or business data on external servers. No account or login is required to use the App.

02

What Data We Collect

InvoiceFlow does not collect personal data in the traditional sense. All information you enter into the App — including your name, business details, client names, invoice data, and financial figures — is stored exclusively on your device.

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact details
  • Invoice data, client information, or financial records
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • Payment information of any kind

The App does not require you to create an account or sign in.

03

How Data is Stored

All data you enter into InvoiceFlow is stored locally on your device using standard browser or app storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it (e.g. saving a PDF to your gallery or sharing it via a messaging app).

Important: If you uninstall the App or clear your browser/app data, your InvoiceFlow data will be permanently deleted. We recommend regularly exporting your invoices as PDFs as a backup.

We do not operate any servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure that stores user data.

04

How We Use Your Data

Since we do not collect your data, we do not use it for any purpose. All processing of your information happens entirely on your own device.

When you use features like PDF Export, the generated PDF is created locally on your device. You choose where to save or share it — we have no involvement in that process.

05

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties because we do not have access to your data.

InvoiceFlow does not contain:

  • Advertising SDKs or ad networks
  • Analytics or tracking tools
  • Third-party data brokers or marketing platforms
  • Social media login integrations

The App is completely ad-free and we have no commercial relationships with data companies.

06

Security

Since your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's own security measures (such as screen lock, encryption, and app permissions).

We recommend:

  • Keeping your device locked with a PIN, pattern, or biometric
  • Keeping your operating system and apps updated
  • Regularly exporting important invoices as PDFs for backup
07

Children's Privacy

InvoiceFlow is designed for use by adults and working professionals. The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

If you believe a child has used the App and you have concerns, please contact us at the email below.

08

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can:

  • Access your data — it is visible within the App at all times
  • Edit your data — update any invoice, client, or profile information directly in the App
  • Delete your data — delete individual invoices or clear all data by uninstalling the App or clearing app storage
  • Export your data — export invoices as PDFs at any time

No request to us is needed to exercise any of these rights, as we do not hold your data.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date at the top.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
